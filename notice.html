Notice regarding the future of the website

On August 2nd 2022, at 3:00 am UTC, the CBT 2 of Goddess of Victory: NIKKE released.

Being a private test, some users couldn't access the game, and tried to find a way to play. Within hours, the apk file of the game started being shared online allowing anyone with a code to play, even for users outside the country range allowed to play the game.

As a response to this issue, the development team of NIKKE wrote two warnings notices on social medias and in game warning users about their wrongdoings.

These warnings are aimed toward users datamining private content of the game and sharing it online, and inform that legal issues may occur for certain cases.

Nikke-DB is taking this threat seriously and will be reorganised to avoid issues.

  1. The website will not show anything NEW from CBT 2 ( characters, skills)
  2. Every characters that are not in CBT 2 will be removed from the website

Nikke-DB will now be what I always wanted it to be, a database of the game and not a leaking platform, while trying to stay on the legal side of Tencent/Proxima Beta/Level Infinite/Shift Up/whoever decided on taking legal actions.

The website may also suddenly disappear one day, if the developers dislike it's existence and gently ( I hope ) ask me to delete everything.

Thank you for your support from everywhere, in English, Korean and Japanese forums. I hope to run this website for as long as I can.
